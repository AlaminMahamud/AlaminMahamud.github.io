Self-Taught

Self-taught are those persons who learn or acquire knowledge on his/her own initiative rather than through formal instruction or training. I learn things all by myself, using notes and resources from all over the internet. As I was studying Mechanical Engineering, I realised that Softwares were my one true passion. Thankfully there were lot's of friends, seniors at campus and teachers who helped me to be on this stage.

Pragmatic - Language Agnostic - Full Stack

Pragmatism is the one true passion of my career. After reading one of the bible of Software Engineering "The Pragmatic Programmer" it created deep impact on how I think and work. Being Language agnostics is the one true way to become pragmatic and I focus on fundamentals primarily which paves the way to become language agnostic.

After spending countless hours practicing on my craft, I now am fully confident to work in any stack I need to.
